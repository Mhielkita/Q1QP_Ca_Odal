SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM

We wanted to know which platforms the respondents like to use the most, like Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok. This helps us understand popular choices. The variable in the spreadsheet is identified as “Social Media” as the variable we are finding in the question is the preferred social media to use of the individual. This is a nominal variable, meaning that the variable is representing something that has no order or ranking. This graph reveals the usage distribution of different social media platforms within the dataset. The most popular platform in the dataset is Facebook, with over half of the individuals (57.3%) using it. Tiktok and Instagram are also quite popular, with 17% and 14% usage respectively. The other platforms have lower usage rates, with some representing less than 1% of the dataset.
